Myung, Chang Woo

@UNIST

I received my PhD at UNIST under the supervision of Prof. Kwang S. Kim. During my PhD, my main research focus was on the first-principles calculation of energy materials (lead halide perovskite solar cells, electrocatalysts, and lithium battery). I am joinimg the group of Prof. Michele Parrinello at ETH Zürich & USI at the end of 2019. My current area of research includes machine learning applications for thermodynamics of materials (fast ab initio molecular dynamics) and quantum chemistry (ground state wavefunction).
